Brief

Rifki is an Indonesian who lives in the city of Patriot, born on October 1st, 2002. He chose to major in computer and network engineering at SMK Negeri 4 Kota Bekasi after graduating from SMP Negeri 4 Kota Bekasi. He is pursuing his bachelor's in computer science and working at BoksMan Asia.

Career

He started his career in technology in 2019 when he was in the first grade of a vocational high school. His career began when he took additional classes in web programming. Then he founded a web programming extracurricular with some of his friends one year later.

He was introduced to an expert programmer by a vocational high school teacher. He continued his career through his support by giving him several projects to complete independently, and he often does projects with his friends to hone his skills.

When he was in second grade at his vocational high school, he was required to do industrial studies to complete his graduation one year later. He and his friends chose to meet his expert programmer (owner of Megonesia) outside of the school and ask him to do it, and as a result, they were allowed to study at PT Bumi Daya Plaza.

In 2021 he graduated from vocational high school and has been employed at PT BoksMan Asia Anugerah Teknologi as a Full Stack Developer.
